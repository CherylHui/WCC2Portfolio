How are you today? (2023)

by Wing Hei Cheryl Hui

An interactive meditation booth.

Introduction

Audience can enter the meditation booth to spend sometime with themselves and meditate.

During the meditation, interactive audiovisual elements will be projected into the meditation booth,

the scent of lavender will also come out of the booth and meditative music will be played during the meditation.

Concept and Background Research

This project is an installation which I took meditation as an inspiration. In the past few years, I was struggling with depression and I learnt to meditate.


I found that meditation helps a lot in controlling my emotion and improving self awareness.


Also, I found that self-love is one of the ways to overcome mental challenges.



I have been living in Hong Kong and now in London, both of the cities are very vibrant, people get used to busy lives and forgot about taking care of themselves.


Therefore, I decided to create an installation which people can take some time to do a very short meditation and at the same time raising the awareness of self love.


For the music creation part, I have studied about the Solfeggio frequencies.


These frequencies are specific tones that claimed to be beneficial towards the mind and body in specific ways.
